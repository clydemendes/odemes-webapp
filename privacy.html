Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Section 01

Information We Collect

Data you create in the app
Odemes stores the data you enter: transactions (income and expenses), categories, and any other information you add. This data is stored on your device and, if you use iCloud, in your personal iCloud account.

We do not collect — We do not collect, sell, or use your personal or financial data for advertising or analytics. We do not require an account with us to use the app.
Section 02

How Your Data Is Stored

  • On your device: Your data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad.
  • iCloud: If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is synced using your own iCloud account. This data is controlled by you and Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.
Section 03

Data We Do Not Access

We do not have servers that store your transactions or personal information. We do not access, read, or transmit your financial data except as necessary to provide app features (e.g., iCloud sync through Apple's services).

Section 04

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are sent by your device based on settings you choose in the app. We do not send push notifications from our own servers or use them to track you.

Section 05

Third Parties

  • Apple: The app uses Apple services (e.g., iCloud). Use of those services is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and terms.
  • No other sharing: We do not share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising.
Section 06

Your Rights

You can:

  • View, edit, and delete your data at any time within the app.
  • Export your data (e.g., via the Export feature in Settings).
  • Disable iCloud Sync or delete the app; your local data is removed with the app (subject to your device and iCloud settings).
Section 07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
